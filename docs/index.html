Welcome to the Librarianshippers Podcast

We're here to add that spark back to your relationship with public libraries

"What's a librarianshipper?" you ask.

According to Urban Dictionary, to ship means wanting "two characters to get together" or showing "support for two characters already together" in a book, on TV, in a video game...even in real life! As students of Library and Information Sciences, we ship you and your public library and are showing our support by discussing all the latest and greatest in library-related current events.

Side note: Urban Dictionary defines "librarian" as "Information Master" and "One to be worshiped," so take that as you will.

The Latest Podcast

November 21, 2021

On this episode of Librarianshippers we discuss artificial intelligence and machine learning—the good, the bad, and the potential. We deep dive into a debate over algorithmic bias and whether libraries and other information spaces can overcome the pitfalls surrounding this issue to harnass the full power of A.I. for the better of all communities. If you think machines are inherently neutral, we're serving up some hot takes that'll blow your mind!
